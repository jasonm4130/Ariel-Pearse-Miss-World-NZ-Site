To my beautiful friends and family!

This is my perfect opportunity to achieve my childhood goal of fighting for my social cause. As a public figure I will be sharing my journey and experiences with everyone on this page!

There are an infinite number of opportunities to make a meaningful difference in this world. The connection I have felt in selfless service has directed my life to have this desire to start my own social enterprise and to champion principles of humanity.

"Remember, you're the one that can fill the world with sunshine."- Snow White... I had the biggest smile as I came out of countdown! My new friend Ace has my respect in two big ways: 1. He had the persistence to learn a talent. 2. He had the courage to show the world! #thesmallthings #hiddentreasures #justdoit

Posted by Ariel Pearse - Miss World New Zealand 2016 Finalist on Tuesday, 8 March 2016
